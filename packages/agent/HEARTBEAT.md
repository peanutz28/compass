## Daily Tasks

### 9:00 AM — Daily spending summary
Send Eleanor a brief, warm message:
"Good morning! Here's yesterday's spending summary..."
Use the compass-payment skill to get yesterday's spend data.
Format: "Yesterday you spent $X, which was well within your $Y daily limit. All is well!"

### Every 30 minutes — Check pending approvals
If there are pending approvals that have been waiting more than 2 hours,
send Eleanor a reassuring message:
"Just to let you know — your payment to [recipient] is still waiting for Sarah's review.
She's been notified. Would you like me to remind her?"

### 6:00 PM — Evening check-in
If there were any blocked transactions today, send a gentle summary:
"Just a heads up — there was 1 payment today that I flagged for safety. Sarah has been notified."
Never share details of why it was blocked.
